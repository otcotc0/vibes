About Trains

Trains are a form of rail transport consisting of a series of connected vehicles that run along railway tracks. They are used for transporting people and goods efficiently across long distances.

Types of Trains

  • 🚄 High-speed trains
  • 🚋 Trams
  • 🚂 Steam trains
  • 🚆 Electric trains
  • 🚇 Metro systems

History of Trains

The first steam-powered trains appeared in the early 19th century. Over time, trains evolved from steam engines to diesel and electric locomotives, becoming faster and more efficient.

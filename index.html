About Trains

Trains are a type of vechicle that run on rails all over the world with lots of different types including steam, diesel, electric, trams, and high speed.

Types of Trains

History of Trains

The first steam-powered trains appeared in the early 19th century. Over time, trains evolved from steam engines to diesel and electric locomotives, becoming faster and more efficient.
